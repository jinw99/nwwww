♡Lomantic places in Central Park♡


  • Night View
  • Moon Boat
  • Cafe

    • Central Park, located in Songdo, Incheon, is a very large park and is one of the places worth visiting if you come to Incheon.

    In Central Park, there is a MoonBoat where you can ride a boat on the river, there is a cafe where you can drink coffee in the garden, and above all, the night view is the best.

    There is a large parking lot and a variety of restaurants nearby, so please come and make pleasant memories
